SAN DIEGO

San Diego is a vibrant coastal city located in Southern California. Known for its beautiful beaches, mild climate, and thriving cultural scene, it is one of the most popular destinations in the state. San Diego boasts a bustling downtown area with shopping, dining, and entertainment options, as well as a variety of neighborhoods that each offer their own unique charm.

Region of state: Southern California Classification of the City: San Diego is classified as an urban city, being the second-largest city in California and having a dense population with a significant metropolitan area.
