SAN FRANCISCO

San Francisco is a vibrant, picturesque city on the northern coast of California known for its iconic landmarks like the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, and steep hills. The city boasts a rich cultural scene, diverse neighborhoods, and a strong technology sector in Silicon Valley. It is a hub for arts, cuisine, and outdoor activities.

Region of state: Southern California Classification of the City: San Francisco is an urban city. It is densely populated and known for its skyscrapers and bustling city life.

SF IS KNOWN FOR

  1. Location - Located northern coast of california, situated on a peninsula surrounded by the pacific ocean and the Bay.
  2. Climate: San Francisco has a cool, foggy Mediterranean climate with mild, wet winters and dry summers. Coastal fog is common, especially in summer.
  3. Demographic: young, highly educated population and a high cost of living
