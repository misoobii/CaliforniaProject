LOS ANGELES

Los Angeles, often known as LA, is the largest city in California and the second-largest in the United States. It's a sprawling urban center located on the Pacific coast, known for its Mediterranean climate, diverse population, and cultural vibrancy. The city is a major global hub for the entertainment industry, particularly in film and television, earning it the nickname "The Entertainment Capital of the World." Hollywood, a district within LA, is synonymous with the movie industry.

Region of state: Southern California Classification of the City: Los Angeles is classified as an urban city, being one of the largest cities in the United States with a dense population and extensive metropolitan area.
